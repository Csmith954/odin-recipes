Taco Recipe

The world's best beef tacos!

The best beef tacos on the planet. An easy to make dish for any night of the week in about 30 mins! Easy to put together taco seasoning with a secret ingredient.

    Things you'll need:
  • Pepper
  • Salt
  • Cumin
  • Chili powder
  • Onion powder
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ground beef
  • Oil
  • Butter
  • Tortillas
    How to make awesome tacos:
  1. Cook the beef until browned, drain the grease. Turn the heat down to low, add the home made seasoning and a small ammount of the tomato sauce.
  2. Simmer the beef in the sauce for a few moments to allow the flavors to blend
  3. Fry the tortillas in the oil on both sides, remove and fold in half to form shape
  4. Fill the shells with the meat and your favorite toppings!
