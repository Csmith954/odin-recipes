Vegan Ice Cream

The best ice cream without the dairy!

Do you love ice cream? Are you a vegan? Do you have dairy intolerance? Well this is the best alternative to the traditional take on ice cream!

    Things needed:
  • cashews
  • coconut milk
  • vanilla bean paste
  • sugar
  • salt
    How to make ice cream:
  1. soak cashews overnight
  2. blend cashews, coconut milk, vanilla bean paste, sugar and salt together until fully smooth
  3. pour mixture into a bowl and place in the fridge for 4 hours
  4. churn ice cream for 20-25 minutes
  5. place in freezer for 4 hours
  6. serve!
