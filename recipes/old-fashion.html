Old Fashion Recipe

The world's greatest cocktail

This drink has been a hit for the last 200 years! Once you sip into this seasoned and sweetened burbon master piece you will understand while it remains relevant today.

    Things you need to get the party started:
  • sugar
  • bitters
  • water
  • burbon
  • orange twist
    How to craft the world's best cocktail:
  1. add one teaspoon of sugar to the bottom of the glass and then add three dashes of the bitters. add in one teaspoonof water and stir until the sugar is disolved.
  2. fill the glass with ice, add 2 ounces of your favorite burbon, and stir until chilled
  3. express the oil of an orange peel over the top of the glass and then rim the glass with the orange peel, drop the the peel into the glass.
